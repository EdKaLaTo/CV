Eddy Ng

co-founder and COO of One Edge Digital

Eddy Ng is the co-founder and Chief Operating Officer of One Edge Digital, an award-winning full-service digital marketing agency based in New York. One Edge Digital assists Fortune 1000 brands and start-up businesses in devising and executing digital media strategies across all digital channels, including Paid Search, SEO, Display, and Social.

As COO, Eddy is responsible for leading the agency's business operations and direction, as well as overseeing digital strategy across teams and media channels. Before founding One Edge, Eddy held numerous executive-level roles at Magnitude, iProspect, Resolution Media (a OMD company) and MediaCom (a WPP/GroupM company). Here, he managed global digital marketing programs for both the agency and key accounts, such as ADT Security, Dell, Lenovo, Sony, Longchamp, University of Southern California, Neato Robotics, Tic Tac, Elizabeth Arden, and Harry Winston.

Eddy is a graduate of Baruch College's Zicklin School of Business. He currently resides in New York City and is fluent in Cantonese. In his free time, Eddy enjoys motorcycling, gaming, and travelling the world to discover new places and cuisine.
